How To Play


* Click 'Start' button to start playing.
* Click on the Red box / Cirlce.
* The location of Box/Cirlce will change. Now press the new location.
* Continue this proccess till the number of times is over.
* A new page shows the Time you took and your best time.

The easy and medium modes are easy. Hard mode requires accuracy & is the best part of the game.
